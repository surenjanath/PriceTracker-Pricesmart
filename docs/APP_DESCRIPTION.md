# PriceAnalysis - App Description

## Overview

PriceAnalysis is a comprehensive mobile application designed to help you track, analyze, and monitor PriceSmart product prices over time. Whether you're a frequent PriceSmart shopper or looking for the best deals, PriceAnalysis provides powerful tools to help you make informed purchasing decisions.

## What Does PriceAnalysis Do?

PriceAnalysis automatically tracks PriceSmart product prices and provides detailed analytics to help you:
- **Monitor Price Changes:** Track how prices change over time for your favorite products
- **Identify Trends:** Spot price patterns and predict the best time to buy
- **Compare Products:** Analyze prices across different categories and brands
- **Get Insights:** Receive daily analysis reports and price change notifications
- **Save Money:** Find the best deals and avoid buying at peak prices

## How It Works

### Data Collection
1. **Automatic Updates:** The app automatically fetches product data from PriceSmart's public website daily at 4 AM
2. **Local Storage:** Product data is cached on your device for fast access
3. **Cloud Sync:** Analytics and preferences are securely synced to our cloud database

### Price Tracking
1. **Historical Data:** Every price change is recorded and stored
2. **Trend Analysis:** Algorithms analyze price patterns to identify trends
3. **Predictions:** Based on historical data, the app can suggest optimal buying times

### Analytics Engine
1. **Real-Time Processing:** Analytics are calculated in real-time as you browse
2. **Aggregated Statistics:** Category and brand statistics are computed from all products
3. **Daily Reports:** Comprehensive daily analysis is generated automatically

## Data Source

### PriceSmart Integration
- **Source:** Public product data from PriceSmart's website
