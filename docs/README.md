## üöÄ Getting Started

### Installation

1. **Download from Google Play Store**
   - Search for "PriceAnalysis"
   - Tap "Install"
   - Wait for installation to complete

2. **Open the App**
   - Tap the app icon on your device
   - Grant necessary permissions when prompted
   - Start exploring!

### First-Time Setup

1. **Initial Load**
   - The app will automatically fetch the latest product data
   - This may take a few moments on first launch
   - Data is cached for faster subsequent loads

2. **Explore Features**
   - Browse products on the Products screen
   - Check out analytics on the Home screen
   - View daily reports in the Analytics section
   - Customize settings to your preference

## üìñ Usage Guide

### Viewing Products

1. **Browse All Products**
   - Navigate to the "Products" tab
   - Scroll through the product list
   - Tap any product for detailed information

2. **Search Products**
   - Use the search bar at the top
   - Type product name, category, or brand
   - Results update in real-time

3. **Filter Products**
   - Tap the filter icon
   - Select category, price range, or availability
   - Apply filters to narrow results

### Analyzing Prices

1. **View Price Trends**
   - Go to Home screen
   - Scroll to "Price Trends" section
   - View 14-day price movement chart

2. **Check Daily Analysis**
   - Navigate to Analytics screen
   - View today's activity summary
   - See biggest price changes

3. **Category Analysis**
   - Home screen ‚Üí Category Analysis section
   - See product distribution by category
   - Compare average prices

### Managing Settings

1. **Privacy Controls**
   - Settings ‚Üí Privacy
   - Enable/disable analytics
   - Manage email subscription

2. **Notifications**
   - Settings ‚Üí Notifications
   - Configure price alerts
   - Set update frequency

3. **Data Refresh**
   - Pull down on Home screen to refresh
   - Or use Settings ‚Üí Refresh Data

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**App won't load data**
- Check your internet connection
- Try pulling down to refresh
- Restart the app

**Prices seem outdated**
- Data updates twice daily (5 AM and 5 PM)
- Pull down to manually refresh
- Check Settings ‚Üí Last Sync time

**App is slow**
- Clear app cache in device settings
- Close other apps to free memory
- Restart your device

**Notifications not working**
- Check app notification permissions
- Verify notifications are enabled in Settings
- Check device notification settings
